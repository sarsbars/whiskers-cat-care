BOARDING

We offer non-kennel boarding featuring two comfortable bedrooms with actual beds and are staffed 24/7. It’s truly a unique experience—that’s why we say, “We’re your cat’s home away from home.”

What To Bring

Favorite Food: Bring enough of your cat’s regular food (including treats or supplements) for the duration of the stay.
Medications & Instructions: If your cat is on any medication, provide the required amount along with clear dosage instructions.
Comfort Items: Pack a favorite blanket, toy, or any item that brings familiarity and eases stress.

Some Mention

Documentation: Include your cat’s vaccination records and any relevant medical history.
Special Needs: Inform our team of any dietary preferences, behavioral quirks, or additional requirements so we can offer personalized care.

Basic Boarding Service

We offer a unique non-kennel boarding experience designed to create a cozy, secure home away from home for your cat. Our facility features two comfortable bedrooms with real beds, and our 24/7 professional care team is always on hand to ensure your pet’s well-being.

Check-In & Daily Care

Before check-in, please reserve in advance and provide your cat’s health certificate and vaccination records. During the stay, we conduct regular health checks and follow a customized dietary plan. Our staff maintains a consistent routine, including scheduled interaction, cleaning, and sanitization to keep your cat comfortable.

Value-Added & Special Care

Beyond our basic services, we offer extras such as grooming, hair care, personalized diet management, and medication administration. For cats with special needs, we create a tailored plan to ensure they receive individualized attention as they adjust to their new environment.
