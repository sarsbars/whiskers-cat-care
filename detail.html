Boarding

Experience our non-kennel boarding with cozy bedrooms, real beds, and 24/7 care—a home away from home for your cat.

What To Bring

Favorite Food: Bring enough of your cat’s regular food (including treats or supplements) for the duration of the stay.

Medications & Instructions: If your cat is on any medication, provide the required amount along with clear dosage instructions.

Comfort Items: Pack a favorite blanket, toy, or any item that brings familiarity and eases stress.

Some Mention

Documentation: Include your cat’s vaccination records and any relevant medical history.

Special Needs: Inform our team of any dietary preferences, behavioral quirks, or additional requirements so we can offer personalized care.

Basic Boarding Service

We offer a unique non-kennel boarding experience designed to create a cozy, secure home away from home for your cat. Our facility features two comfortable bedrooms with real beds, and our 24/7 professional care team is always on hand to ensure your pet’s well-being.

Check-In Process & Daily Care

Before check-in, please reserve in advance and provide your cat’s health certificate and vaccination records. During the stay, we conduct regular health checks and follow a customized dietary plan. Our staff maintains a consistent daily routine, including scheduled interaction, cleaning, and environmental sanitization to keep your cat comfortable.

Value-Added Services & Special Care

Beyond our basic services, we offer extras such as grooming, hair care, personalized diet management, and medication administration guidance. For cats with special needs, we create a tailored care plan to ensure they receive individualized attention as they adjust to their new environment.

Additional services, including grooming, medication management, and custom meals, are charged separately.
