About Us

Whiskers is a cat care and grooming service built with love for every feline. We offer gentle, personalized grooming that keeps your cat looking and feeling great. Our team is trained to treat each cat with calm, care, and kindness because happy cats make happy homes.

Our services

We recognize that your schedule can get hectic, so we offer a wide selection of services designed to simplify life for you and your cherished pets.

Daycare

Our cat daycare service is a favorite among pet parents who value both safety and enrichment. We ensure your feline friends have plenty of playtime, mental stimulation, and cozy spots to lounge, so they remain happy and relaxed throughout the day.

Boarding

When you’re away, your cat can enjoy our homelike boarding facility, staffed round the clock to provide personalized attention. Each feline guest gets a snug sleeping area, fun activities, and lots of gentle care, ensuring they feel comfortable and secure until you return.

Grooming

Your cat’s well-being is at the heart of our grooming service. We take the time to understand each kitty’s preferences, offering gentle brushing, nail trimming, and optional pampering treatments. Rest assured, we always handle your feline companion with kindness and patience—snuggles included.

Training

Our cat training program is a favorite among pet parents who value both playfulness and discipline. We utilize gentle, positive reinforcement techniques to guide your feline friend in learning essential tricks and manners, ensuring a well-behaved, confident companion.
